Proposal: Development and Security Plan for Gulistan, Paltan, Motijheel, and Dhaka South City Corporation

Proposer: Md. Zafar Ahmad, Founder Director, Quran Printing Institution

Introduction:

The areas of Gulistan, Paltan, and Motijheel in Dhaka are the economic and administrative hubs of the country. In the current situation, it is crucial to ensure the development and security of these areas. As a Microsoft web developer and a member of various international communities, I wish to contribute to the development of these areas using my skills and experience.

Objectives:

  • To restore economic activities in the Gulistan, Paltan, and Motijheel areas.
  • To normalize the activities of government offices and other important institutions.
  • To improve the law and order situation.
  • To honor the freedom and sacrifices achieved through student movements.
  • To develop Dhaka South City Corporation as a model city for international standards in education, security, commerce, and normal living.

Plan:

1. Creation of a Digital Platform:

  • Develop a website and mobile application to monitor the activities of government offices and other institutions.
  • Through this platform, citizens can report their problems and get solutions.
  • Microsoft Azure: A cloud computing platform that will provide data storage and processing capabilities.
  • Power BI: A data visualization and analysis tool that will assist in decision-making.
  • GitHub: A code hosting platform that will facilitate collaboration among developers.

2. Enhancement of Security Measures:

  • Install CCTV cameras and create a monitoring center.
  • Strengthen security measures in coordination with the police and other law enforcement agencies.
  • Microsoft Security Center: Will help identify and prevent security threats.
  • AI-based Facial Recognition: Will be used to identify criminals and enhance security.
  • Azure Sentinel: A cloud-native security information and event management (SIEM) tool that will help identify and prevent security threats.

3. Revival of Economic Activities:

  • Provide special incentives and support for businesses.
  • Take necessary measures to quickly resume the activities of government offices and other institutions.
  • Microsoft Dynamics 365: Will improve business operations and customer relationship management.
  • Power Automate: Will enhance process automation and efficiency.
  • Azure Blockchain: Will ensure secure and transparent transactions.

4. Special Initiatives for Students:

  • Launch special scholarship and training programs for students.
  • Take special initiatives to honor the freedom and sacrifices achieved through student movements.
  • Microsoft Education Tools: Will create an advanced education system for students.
  • Microsoft Teams: Will be used for online classes and collaborative learning.
  • Azure Lab Services: A virtual lab environment that will provide practical training for students.

5. Transforming Dhaka South City Corporation into an International Standard City:

  • Take special initiatives to develop the city as a model for education, security, commerce, and normal living.
  • Microsoft HoloLens: Will use augmented reality (AR) technology to plan and implement city development.
  • Microsoft Surface Devices: Modern technology and equipment that will assist in administrative activities.
  • Azure Digital Twins: Will create a digital model of the city to monitor development planning and implementation.

6. Increasing Awareness and Cooperation in Tax Collection:

  • Form various teams with the help of students to conduct extensive campaigns among businesses during school holidays.
  • Educate businesses on the importance of paying taxes and how it contributes to national economic development.
  • Microsoft Power Platform: Will be used to conduct and monitor campaigns and awareness activities.
  • AI and Machine Learning: Will enhance the transparency and efficiency of the tax collection process.
  • Azure Customer Insights: Will analyze the tax payment trends and behaviors of businesses to take effective measures.

Conclusion:

Through this proposal and project plan, I hope to ensure the development and security of the Gulistan, Paltan, and Motijheel areas. I am ready to contribute to this initiative using all my skills and experience. Through this initiative, I wish to pay tribute to those who have sacrificed their lives for the country.

I would be grateful for your kind consideration.

Sincerely,
Md. Zafar Ahmad
Founder Director,
Quran Printing Institution
