Questions

Is this donation tax deductible?

Creative Commons Corporation is a 501(c)3 tax-exempt organization and your donation is tax-deductible within the guidelines of U.S. law. To claim a donation as a deduction on your U.S. taxes, please keep your email donation receipt as your official record. We'll send it to you upon successful completion of your donation.

Can I donate via Paypal?

Yes! You can make a secure, private donation to CC here: https://www.paypal.com/donate/?hosted_button_id=RHB7C3BUX9646
Please note that the selected currency must be USD.

Can I donate crypto?

Yes! Creative Commons accepts cryptocurrency donations through Classy powered by Coinbase. See our Donor FAQ below for other cryptocurrency giving options.
